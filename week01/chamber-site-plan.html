Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to give visibility to the products we offer in our family venture at a regional level. In this regard, we aim to showcase the individual presentations of each product and the option to combine different products in a box that becomes a useful gift throughout all seasons of the year.

Scenarios

Our audience consists of all those who enjoy cooking and use seasonings from both Chilean and international cuisine in their recipes. Additionally, due to the presentations of our products, they can also be used as gifts for family members passionate about Chilean or international cuisine.
